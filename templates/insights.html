90-Day Rolling Correlation: GLD vs SLV & SPX
GLD ↔ SLV

Silver and gold are both precious metals. Correlation stays high (~0.8–0.95) because they respond to the same macro forces: inflation, USD weakness, safe-haven demand.

GLD ↔ SPX

S&P 500 vs gold correlation fluctuates around zero and goes negative during crises. This confirms gold's safe-haven role — when stocks crash, investors flee to gold.
